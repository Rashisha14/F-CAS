Film Censorship Automation System (F-CAS)

AI-Based Film Censoring System

Abstract:

This project introduces an AI-driven system designed to revolutionize the film censorship process by leveraging advanced machine learning and computer vision techniques. The system automatically identifies and flags specific frames containing depictions of alcohol consumption, drug use, or other restricted content, providing precise timestamps of these occurrences to enable efficient review and editing. By integrating with editing tools, it expedites the removal or modification of flagged content, reducing the manual effort involved in reviewing lengthy footage. This approach ensures high accuracy, minimizes human error, and promotes adherence to region-specific censorship laws, offering a transformative solution for the film industry.
